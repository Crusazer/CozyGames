Forum Code of Conduct

  1. Respect Others: Treat fellow forum members with kindness and respect. Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or any other characteristic.
  2. Be Civil and Constructive: Keep discussions civil and focused on constructive dialogue. Disagreements are natural, but express your viewpoints respectfully without resorting to hostility or insults.
  3. Stay on Topic: When participating in discussions, stay on topic and avoid derailing threads with unrelated content. If you have a new topic to discuss, start a new thread rather than hijacking existing ones.
  4. No Spam or Self-Promotion: Avoid spamming the forum with irrelevant or promotional content. Self-promotion should be limited and relevant to the discussion.
  5. Respect Privacy: Do not share personal information about yourself or others without consent. Respect the privacy of fellow forum members.
  6. Follow Guidelines and Instructions: Adhere to any specific guidelines or instructions provided by forum moderators. Failure to comply may result in moderation actions.
  7. Use Appropriate Language and Content: Keep language and content appropriate for all audiences. Avoid offensive, obscene, or inappropriate language, images, or links.
  8. Report Misconduct: If you encounter any behavior that violates these rules or makes you feel uncomfortable, report it to forum moderators for appropriate action.
  9. Consequences of Violations: Violations of the forum code of conduct may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans from the forum, depending on the severity and frequency of the offense.
