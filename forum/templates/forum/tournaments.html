Tournaments

Welcome to our tournaments page! Here you can participate in exciting competitions of tabletop games or create your own tournament. We offer various types of tournaments, including open and closed ones.

Open Tournaments: Anyone interested can join open tournaments without any restrictions. Simply choose the tournament you're interested in and apply to participate!

Closed Tournaments: Some tournaments may be available by invitation only or after confirmation by the creator. If you have an invitation or want to create your own closed tournament, please contact the site administrator or create your tournament with the appropriate settings.

Remember that participation in tournaments implies adherence to the general rules of conducting competitions:

  1. Respect Others: Treat fellow participants with kindness and respect. Avoid insults, discrimination, or any other form of intolerance.
  2. Be Civil and Constructive: Maintain polite and constructive dialogue. Disagreements are normal, but express your viewpoints respectfully, without hostility or insults.
